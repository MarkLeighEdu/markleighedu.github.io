<h2>Insights</h2>
<strong style="color: #2caef7;">USD INDEX</strong> Yesterday's pause was followed up with sellers making a strong push lower resulting in price action back bellow the 100.00 mark. Bearing in mind the recent strong performance of the USD, the turnaround can be seen as a natural retracement, coupled with a bout of profit taking as opposed to a shift in (positive) sentiment towards the USD. Resistance at 99.93 with support at 99.49.
